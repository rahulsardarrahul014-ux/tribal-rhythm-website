📜 Culture History

Santali Culture Bhumij Culture

Santali culture rich tribal tradition hai. Dance, music, festivals jaise Karam, Sohrai famous hai.

Santali culture Bharat ke sabse purane aur samriddh tribal cultures me se ek hai, jo mainly Jharkhand, Odisha, West Bengal aur Bihar ke ilaakon me paya jata hai. Santali samaj prakriti se gahra juda hua hai, aur unki jeevan shailee forest, nadi, aur zameen ke aas-paas ghoomti hai. Unke paramparik tyohar jaise KARAM, SOHRAI, aur BAHA unki sanskriti ka mahatvapurn hissa hain, jahan gaon ke log ek saath milkar dance, music aur pooja karte hain. Santali dance aur music bahut hi energetic hote hain, jisme dhol, madal aur flute jaise traditional instruments ka use hota hai. Unki bhasha, Santali, apni alag pehchaan rakhti hai aur Ol Chiki script me likhi jati hai. Samaj me ek strong community system hota hai jahan sab log milkar faisle lete hain aur paramparaon ka palan karte hain. Santali log apni pehchaan aur virasat ko bahut mahatva dete hain, aur naye zamane ke saath judte hue bhi apni sanskriti ko sanrakshit rakhne ki koshish kar rahe hain. Yeh culture unity, nature respect aur traditional values ka ek sundar udaharan hai.

Bhumij culture nature based aur traditional hai. Festivals aur community dance iska main part hai.

Bhumij culture ek prachin aur samriddh tribal culture hai jo mainly Jharkhand, West Bengal, Odisha aur Bihar ke kuch ilaakon me paya jata hai. Bhumij samaj ka jeevan prakriti se juda hota hai, jahan jungle, zameen aur jal unke jeevan ka important hissa hote hain. Unki sanskriti me parampara, samajik ekta aur prakriti ka samman sabse upar rakha jata hai. Bhumij log apne traditional festivals jaise Karam, TUSU, aur anya seasonal tyoharon ko bade utsah ke saath manate hain. In tyoharon me gaon ke log ek saath milkar dance, gaan aur pooja karte hain. Unka dance aur music bahut hi energetic hota hai, jisme dhol, nagara aur anya local instruments ka use hota hai. Bhumij samaj me ek strong community system hota hai jahan bade buzurg faisle lete hain aur sab log unka samman karte hain. Unki bhasha aur parampara unki pehchaan ko banaye rakhti hai. Aaj ke modern samay me bhi Bhumij log apni virasat aur parampara ko sambhal kar rakhe hue hain aur naye yug ke saath chalne ki koshish kar rahe hain. Yeh culture simplicity, unity aur nature ke prati gehra respect dikhata hai.
